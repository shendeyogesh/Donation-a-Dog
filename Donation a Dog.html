About

The already sad state of stray animals in our cities paired with the current scenario in light of COVID-19 with fewer volunteers and resources available to feed. Them has made it important for us to contribute now more than ever. We support individuals and organizations working towards the welfare of animals with food supply. Dog Donation Rescue assists approximately 2,000 animals per year to keep the population of strays healthy and community of Dog free of rabies. Some dogs cannot be put back out on the street and do not get adopted, therefore we need to give them a forever home with DAR. We need your help to ensure we have enough funds to make their lives matter. Once you selected the donation amount you would like to contribute, please click on the green “MAKE A DONATION” button. From there you will be directed to the cart and checkout page where payment can be made via PayPal or Credit Card. The payment is not recurring and we won’t store any card details. Contact us if in doubt or for other payment options
